Leadership Experiences

Background

I have always been interested in extracurricular activities since high school, where I served as the Student Body Vice President, TEDx Organization Leader, Rotary Interact Club Leader, and was a member of the Community Leader's Club, an exclusive group for students invited by faculty.

My enthusiasm for extracurricular experiences did not diminish when I joined Boston University, as I continued to hold several leadership positions since my freshman year.

Participating in extracurricular activities has been a source of immense fascination and personal growth for me.

These pursuits have allowed me to venture beyond the confines of traditional coursework, nurturing my ability to think creatively and innovatively.

As I continue to engage in various extracurricular endeavors, I eagerly anticipate exploring diverse areas of interest and expanding my horizons.
