Projects

Laparoscopy Project

  • Project led by Lorenzo Kinnicutt, a Ph.D. candidate at Boston University.
  • Time Commitment: January 2022 - May 2023 (In-person)
  • Skills: Prototyping, Python, MATLAB, CAD (Solidworks)
  • Summary:
    • Molded silicone pneumatic actuators that provide reliable grips on abdominal organs during surgical procedures and conducted various strength tests, including blocked force and grip testing, to assess actuators' reliability

Miniaturized Fiber-Reinforced Soft Actuator

  • The Fiber-Reinforced Soft Actuator plays a pivotal role in our project.
  • We plan to employ this actuator to manipulate the small intestine due to its soft and flexible nature.
  • I was the primary researcher who, with the assistance of Lorenzo Kinnicutt, prototyped this actuator on a small scale and conducted extensive material testing.
